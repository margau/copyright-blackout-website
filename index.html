You have the chance to keep this freedom!

To protest against the planned EU copyright directive, which would have an large impact at nearly all, especially the small, internet platforms, we will simulate how the internet could look if the directive passes the EU-parliament.

Many platforms, including the german Wikipedia, will turn dark on March 21!

What's the problem?

With article 13 and other parts of the directive, small and medium-sized plattforms will be forced to implement uploadfilters or similar. For much platforms and boards, this is beyond their capabilities, and their existence is in danger!

What to do against it?

  • Sign the petition
  • Go to the Demonstration on March 23
  • Mail your MdEP
  • Call you MdEP
  • Write a letter to your MdEP
